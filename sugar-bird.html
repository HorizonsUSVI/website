Sugar Bird Cottage

1 Bedroom with A/C, 1.5 Baths, and 3 Levels of Decks – All with Ocean Views!

Sugar Bird is a secluded cottage with amazing, panoramic views of the British Virgin Islands and the East end of St John. Perched above Johns Folly Bay, the wrap-around decks on the top floor provide space for outdoor dining and lounging, all with unbeatable views! The gentle trade winds make the private decks a perfect place to relax, day or night.

The Views & Decks

The gentle trade winds make the private decks a perfect place to relax, day or night. Watch the amazing sun and moon rises, or enjoy fantastic star gazing and bird watching.

Upper Level Living

Large picture windows provide unobstructed views. Screened louvers are cleverly placed below the windows so the comfortable breeze can naturally cool the cottage without obscuring your view.

The kitchen has a stovetop, oven, full-sized refrigerator, microwave, coffee maker, and all the supplies you'll need to feel at home. Also upstairs is a dining area, a half-bath, a couch and smart TV.

Bedroom Suite & Lower Decks

On the lower level is the air-conditioned bedroom with an ensuite bath and a shower big enough for two – with a view! There's also a covered deck off the bedroom, and a third, lower deck perfect for sunbathing.

Private Outdoor Shower

Enjoy the private, tropical outdoor shower.

Easy Access

The cottage is located near the end of a quiet, private, paved road. No traffic noise or bumpy, steep, rocky roads that require 4-wheel drive.

Cottage Access

Please note that there are a few flights of steps down to the cottage and decks, so mobility is required.
