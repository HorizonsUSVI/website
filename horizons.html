Horizons Cottage

2 Bedrooms, 2-1/2 Baths, and 2 Decks – with Ocean Views from Every Room!

Horizons Cottage is a peaceful and private, open-concept home with amazing, panoramic views of the British Virgin Islands. Perched above Johns Folly Bay, the wrap-around deck provides plenty of space and unbeatable views!

Open Concept Living, Dining & Kitchen

Large picture windows provide unobstructed views from inside. The space seamlessly combines the living area, dining for four, and a fully equipped kitchen with granite countertops and breakfast bar.

The Bedrooms

Convenient interior stairs lead to the lower level where both bedrooms have ensuite bathrooms.

First Bedroom

King bed, ensuite bathroom, and plenty of storage.

Second Bedroom

Two twins (convertible to King upon request) with ensuite bathroom.

Tropical Screened Showers

Both downstairs bathrooms have unique, completely screened tropical showers. Experience the island breeze with complete privacy.

Outdoor Living

A huge wrap-around deck surrounds the upper level, offering space for dining, lounging, and hammocking.

The Views & Surroundings

Eastern exposure allows for beautiful sunrises and cool, shaded afternoons. The cottage is private, quiet, and located on a paved road.
