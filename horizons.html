Horizons Cottage

2 Bedrooms, 2-1/2 Baths, and 2 Decks – with Ocean Views from Every Room!

Horizons Cottage is a peaceful and private, open-concept home with amazing, panoramic views of the British Virgin Islands and the East end of St John, near beautiful Coral Bay, St. John, USVI. Perched above Johns Folly Bay, the wrap-around deck provides plenty of space and unbeatable views!

The Great Room

Large picture windows provide unobstructed views from inside. The space seamlessly combines the living area, dining for four, and a fully equipped kitchen with granite countertops.

The Bedrooms

Convenient interior stairs lead to the lower bedroom level where both bedrooms have ensuite bathrooms.

First Bedroom

Large room with King bed, louvered windows, and ensuite bathroom.

Second Bedroom

Two twins (can be made into a king if requested), also with an ensuite bathroom.

Tropical Screened Showers

Both downstairs bathrooms have unique, completely screened, tropical showers.

Outdoor Living

The upper level is surrounded with a huge, wrap-around deck. There’s enough space for outdoor dining and lounging.

Privacy & Views

The eastern exposure allows for cool, shaded afternoons and beautiful sunrises. The cottage is located near the end of a quiet, private, paved road.

Amenities

  • 2 Bedrooms, sleeps 4: 1 king, 2 twins (convertible to king).
  • 3 Bathrooms: 1/2 bath upstairs, 2 ensuite with screened showers.
  • High speed Starlink internet/wi-fi with battery back-up.
  • High quality linens and towels.
  • Filtered, UV-treated, potable water.
  • Easy, accessible parking for two vehicles (Paved road).
  • Smoke detectors, fire extinguishers, exterior safety lighting.
  • Fully equipped kitchen: Granite countertops, dishwasher, oven, stove.
  • Microwave, toaster oven, refrigerator with ice maker.
  • Outdoor gas grill.
  • Dining table for 4 & Couches for 5.
  • Samsung Frame TV & Assortment of books.
  • Beach chairs, beach towels, and cooler.
  • Nearby: Snorkeling, Hiking, Restaurants (10 mins).

Horizons Cottage is a peaceful getaway – surrounded by nature, the sound of the ocean below, and expansive views. We invite you to spend an unforgettable vacation at our cottage!
